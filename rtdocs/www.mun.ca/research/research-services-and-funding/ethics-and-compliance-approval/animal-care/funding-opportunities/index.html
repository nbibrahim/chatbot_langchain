Funding opportunities

Funding is available for through Canadian and International laboratory animal groups to conduct research in areas that is aimed at achieving a greater understanding of and improvements to research animal well-being. Examples of projects previously funded by these organizations include studies into optimal environmental enrichment products, housing, and nutrition. For further information, please contact acs@mun.ca.
