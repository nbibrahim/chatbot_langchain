Grant Facilitators & Research Officers

Grant Facilitators & Research Officers

Grants facilitators and research officers based in academic units provide a point of contact between researchers in an academic unit and Research & Initiatives Services (RIS). They regularly work with RIS to ensure researchers are appropriately familiar with university and sponsoring agencies' policies, guidelines, and deadlines.
