Contact us

The Registrar's Office serves prospective students, current Memorial students and the University community, from admissions, academic advising, registration, student records, final examinations and graduation.

Frequently requested services

Important dates and University Diary

Important dates for the academic year, including exams and registration periods, semester breaks, holidays and start/end dates for each semester.

Registration

Review the details you'll need to register for courses at Memorial and check out the step-by-step videos.

Course offerings 

Check out the list of courses available for registration.

Transfer credits

Receive transfer credits from a previous institution or request permission (as a current student) to take courses at another institution.

Academic advising

Unsure of your academic path? Get advice from an advisor on planning, choosing, declaring and/or changing your degree program.

Enrolment verification

Request letters that confirm your enrolment at Memorial: confirmation of enrolment, attendance/credential verification and post-graduate work permits.

Final exams and exam schedule

Check your exam schedule and learn more about final exams, including exam conflicts, deferrals, exemptions and examinaton re-reads.

Grades, averages and GPA

Learn how your average and GPA is calculated.

Degree audits

Request a degree/program audit to ensure you're on track and are taking the appropriate courses to complete your degree.

Transcripts

Get a copy of your official transcript sent to you via MyCreds.

Graduation

Learn more about your eligibility to graduate, receive your degree or replacing your parchment.
