Undergraduate academic advising contacts

St. John's campus

The Academic Advising Centre and faculty/school advisors on the St. John’s campus are available to support you in your academic journey.

Current students declared in a program can book an advising session with an advisor from their faculty/school via the Navigate Student app.

Undeclared students or students considering a change to their program can book an appointment using Navigate Student (select "choosing/changing your program" as the appointment service) or you can send an email to advice@mun.ca.
