Rehearsal & Performance Spaces

Do you require rehearsal or performance space? The School of Music provides a range of rehearsal and performance spaces.

Find out more about the School of Music's rental rates and fees  or for information on facility availability and capabilities, please contact the Concert and Facilities Coordinator Mark Micklethwaite.

You may find other rehearsal spaces here.

Additionally, there is rehearsal space at the St. John's Arts and Culture Centre (ACC) which is located on campus. These spaces are for creation and rehearsal only. To request space at the ACC email admin@artsandculturecentre.com and identify what specifically the request is for.
