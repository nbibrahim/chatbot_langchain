Social/Other Spaces

Are you planning a reception or other social event? St. John's Campus has several suitable venues for any occasion.  From May to August, we close down academic activities and open our doors for your summer plans.

Are you hosting a faith-group gathering? Memorial's Chapel is the perfect venue for gatherings of faith-groups, worship and other religious activities. Looking for a place to hold a wedding? Gushue Hall can be transformed for just that.  Browse the list below to find one of many spaces that may fit your needs. 

Other rooms/atriums/spaces are available throughout campus . If you are interested in a space not listed, please contact the general office of that building to find out more about the space.  

Capacity is listed as standing / seated.
