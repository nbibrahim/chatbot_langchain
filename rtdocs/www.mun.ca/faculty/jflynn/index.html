Dr. Jennifer Flynn

I am Associate Professor of Bioethics in the Division of Community Health and Humanities, Faculty of Medicine, and am a core faculty member in our newly established Centre for Bioethics. As a moral philosopher and bioethicist, my research interests include meta-ethics, normative ethics, reproduction, and clinical ethics.
