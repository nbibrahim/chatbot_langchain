Bruce Watson

Professor of Mathematics (retired)

PhD Western Ontario, 1974
M.A. Western Ontario, 1969

bruce2@mun.ca

Personal website
My mathematical interests include classical summability theory and fixed point theory. On the pedagogical side, I am interested in how technology can be incorporated into or grafted onto the calculus curriculum. This includes innovative uses of symbolic algebra packages (especially DERIVE), symbolic calculators (like the TI-89) and the Web as a delivery tool.
