Each May the School of Graduate Studies holds a Graduate Awards Ceremony. This ceremony is to honour the Fellows of the School of Graduate Studies and the recipients of other major scholarships and awards for the previous scholarship year.

This event recognizes not only the academic excellence shown by our award recipients, but also the generous donors who made these awards possible. It also provides the opportunity for the university community to personally congratulate the awards recipients.

The School of Graduate Studies would like to acknowledge the various sponsors and agencies whose generous donations have greatly enhanced graduate studies at Memorial University.

Each May the School of Graduate Studies holds a Graduate Awards Ceremony. This ceremony is to honour the Fellows of the School of Graduate Studies and the recipients of other major scholarships and awards for the previous scholarship year.

This event recognizes not only the academic excellence shown by our award recipients, but also the generous donors who made these awards possible. It also provides the opportunity for the university community to personally congratulate the awards recipients.

The School of Graduate Studies would like to acknowledge the various sponsors and agencies whose generous donations have greatly enhanced graduate studies at Memorial University.

The School of Graduate Studies would like to acknowledge the various sponsors and agencies whose generous donations have greatly enhanced graduate studies at Memorial University.

Each May the School of Graduate Studies holds a Graduate Awards Ceremony. This ceremony is to honour the Fellows of the School of Graduate Studies and the recipients of other major scholarships and awards for the previous scholarship year.

This event recognizes not only the academic excellence shown by our award recipients, but also the generous donors who made these awards possible. It also provides the opportunity for the university community to personally congratulate the awards recipients.

The School of Graduate Studies would like to acknowledge the various sponsors and agencies whose generous donations have greatly enhanced graduate studies at Memorial University.

Each May the School of Graduate Studies holds a Graduate Awards Ceremony. This ceremony is to honour the Fellows of the School of Graduate Studies and the recipients of other major scholarships and awards for the previous scholarship year.

This event recognizes not only the academic excellence shown by our award recipients, but also the generous donors who made these awards possible. It also provides the opportunity for the university community to personally congratulate the awards recipients.

The School of Graduate Studies would like to acknowledge the various sponsors and agencies whose generous donations have greatly enhanced graduate studies at Memorial University.

Each May the School of Graduate Studies holds a Graduate Awards Ceremony. This ceremony is to honour the Fellows of the School of Graduate Studies and the recipients of other major scholarships and awards for the previous scholarship year.

This event recognizes not only the academic excellence shown by our award recipients, but also the generous donors who made these awards possible. It also provides the opportunity for the university community to personally congratulate the awards recipients.

The School of Graduate Studies would like to acknowledge the various sponsors and agencies whose generous donations have greatly enhanced graduate studies at Memorial University.
