Welcome to Memorial University’s new graduate student blog!

October 9, 2015 by School of Graduate Studies

Are you interested in finding out what graduate school at Memorial University is really like? Well stay tuned! Six new graduate students will be blogging about their first-hand experiences as new graduate students. Follow their journey throughout the first year of their program and get an inside look at graduate life at Memorial.

Let’s introduce our student bloggers:

Meghan Loch is from Bedford, Nova Scotia, Canada and is completing a Master of Arts in English.

Chris Lively is from Truro, Nova Scotia, Canada and is completing a Master of Science in Experimental Psychology.

Elena Salogni is from Passirano, Brescia, Italy, and is completing a PhD in Biology.

Natasha LeClerc is from Ottawa, Ontario, Canada and is completing a Master of Arts in Archaeology.

Lauranne Poharec is from Spézet, Brittany, France and is completing a PhD in English.

Jennifer Dare is from Churchill, Ontario, Canada and is completing a Master of Arts in Environmental Policy.
