Graduate officers and admin staff

As a graduate student at Memorial, a number of faculty and staff members are here to help you at every step of your journey.

Every academic unit has a graduate officer (usually a faculty member) and graduate administrative staff who oversee all aspects of graduate program administration, including admissions, course offerings, non-course requirements, student progress, fellowships budget, scholarships competitions, research exams, and graduation. You’ll find their contact information below.

Please do not hesitate to reach out to either if you have questions or concerns. For more general inquiries, you can also contact the faculty and staff in the School of Graduate Studies.
