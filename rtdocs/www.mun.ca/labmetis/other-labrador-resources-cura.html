Other Labrador Resources

The following list of publications, theses and other sources provides further information regarding other Labrador resources.


Boudreau, Stéphane, S. Payette, C. Morneau and S. Couturier

2003   Recent Decline of the George River Caribou Herd as Revealed by Tree-Ring Analysis. Arctic, Antarctic, and Alpine Research 35(2): 187-195.


Cartwright, George

1792   A Journal of Transactions and Events During a Residence of Nearly Sixteen Years on the Coast of Labrador. Allin and Ridge, Newark.


Curtis, R.

1774   Particulars of the Country of Labradore, Extracted from the Papers of the Lieutenant Roger Curtis, of his Majesty's Sloop the Otter, with a Plane Chart of the Coast. Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society 64: 372-388.


Curwen, Eliot.

1996   Labrador Odyssey: The Journal and Photographs of Eliot Curwen on the Second Voyage of Wilfred Grenfell, 1893. Ronald Rompkey ed. Queen's-McGill Press, Montreal.


Gosling, William G.

1910   Labrador: its discovery, exploration, and development. Alston Rivers , London. Web version: http://www.ourroots.ca/e/toc.aspx?id=1205 ( also have pdf)


Hind, Henry Youle

1863   Explorations in the Interior of the Labrador Peninsula, Vol. I. Longman, Green, Longman, Roberts & Green, London.


Kohlmeister, Benjamin & G. Kmoch

1814   Journal Of A Voyage From Okkak, On The Coast Of Labrador, Ungava Bay, Westward Of Cape Chudleigh; Undertaken To Explore The Coast, And Visit The Esquimaux In That Unknown Region. W. M'Dowall, London.


Rompkey, Ronald (editor)

1996   Labrador Odyssey: The Journal and Photographs of Eliot Curwen on the Second Voyage of Wilfred Grenfell, 1893. Queen's-McGill Press, Montreal.


Townsend, Charles Wendell (editor)

1911   Captain Cartwright and his Labrador journal. D. Estes & company, Boston.
