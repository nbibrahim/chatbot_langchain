50th Anniversary Booklet

Back in 2016, when we were still the School of Nursing, we celebrated our 50th Anniversary. It was a big deal and to mark the occassion we held a number of special events over one weekend in October, welcoming alumni from around the province, across the country, and internationally. We also producced a 50th Anniversary booklet documenting in pictures and text our origin story, and highights over 50 years of educating nurses.
