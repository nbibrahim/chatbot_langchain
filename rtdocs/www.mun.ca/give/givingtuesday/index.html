GivingTuesday

DONATE NOW

Join us between Nov. 26 and 28, 2023, when Memorial University is once again harnessing the power of community in a mission to support our students and help them reach their full potential during their time at Memorial.   

Double your impact  

Thanks to a special group of donors, our GivingTuesday Champions, donations made between Nov. 26 and 28, 2023 will be matched up to $55,000 with a donation to the Thriving Student Fund. A $50 donation will have the impact of $100! 

This GivingTuesday, let’s make a difference, together! No matter where you direct your gift, 100% of your donation will go to the area you choose to support and benefit Memorial's students, their education and future.   

What is GivingTuesday?  

GivingTuesday is a global movement that inspires generosity and kindness. Following on the heels of Black Friday and Cyber Monday, it kicks off the giving season and encourages people to do good in their communities.  

Memorial has been participating in GivingTuesday since 2015. Since then, alumni, faculty, staff, students and friends have raised over $360,000, showing that, together, we make a real difference for our students!  

Thriving Student Fund  

The Thriving Student Fund was created as a priority fund for GivingTuesday to provide funding as needed, where needed for students. Whether they need short-term emergency support, or by empowering their success through scholarships and bursaries, this fund helps students to achieve academic and personal success during their time at Memorial University.   
  
All donations to this fund will support undergraduate and graduate students across all campuses, increasing access to education and providing financial support so that students can focus on their studies.   

Share your #UNselfie  

As part of Memorial’s GivingTuesday event, we invite you to take part in our #UNselfie campaign and post your personal message of support on social media. 

Here’s how you can share your giving story and motivate others to give:  

  1. Download and print our #MemorialUGiving #UNselfie template in colour or in black & white:
    - UNselfie General, B&W
    - UNselfie General, Colour
    - UNselfie Marine Institute, B&W
    - UNselfie Marine Institute, Colour
    - UNselfie Grenfell, B&W
    - UNselfie Grenfell, Colour
    - UNselfie Labrador Campus, B&W
    - UNselfie Labrador Campus, Colour

  2. Write how you’re making a difference on this special day (planning to volunteer, donating to a cause, supporting a specific fund at Memorial University, etc.).

  3. Say cheese! Snap your #UNselfie photo, share it on your social media channels and inspire others to open their hearts and tap into their giving spirit.

  4. Don't forget to tag @MemorialU, and use the hashtags #UNselfie, #GivingTuesdayCA and #MemorialUGiving! If you're sharing from Grenfell, MI or Labrador Campus, please feel free to tag @MemorialU and add the hashtags #GrenfellGiving, #MIGiving and #LabradorGiving to your posts.

  5. We can't wait to see your smiles!  

Join us this GivingTuesday, between Nov. 26 and 28, by making a donation to the Thriving Student Fund or any other area at Memorial to have your gift counted and matched!
