Student Opportunities - Community

Previously Hosted Students - Community  
  • AIDS Committee of Newfoundland and Labrador: Promoting the prevention of HIV/AIDS and Hepatitis C, as well as supporting those living with either disorder. Connections with the Department of Psychology, the Faculty of Medicine, the School of Pharmacy, and the School of Social Work. Contact: 709-579-8656.
  • Association for New Canadians: Provide support to newcomers and help them integrate to Newfoundland and Labrador. Connections with the Department of Political Science, the Faculty of Medicine, the School of Pharmacy, Student Life, and the Student Volunteer Bureau. Contact: 709-722-9680 (local) or 1-833-222-0921 (toll-free).
  • Autism Society of Newfoundland and Labrador: Raising awareness about autism spectrum disorders, as well as advocating for and supporting those living with an autism spectrum disorder. Connections with the School of Social Work, Student Life, and the Student Volunteer Bureau. Contact: info@autism.nf.net, 709-722-2803.
    • NL locations with contact information.
  • Boys & Girls Clubs of Canada: Aim to meet the needs of young people in Newfoundland and Labrador and nationally. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine and the Student Volunteer Bureau. Contact: 709-579-0181 (St. John's).
    • Search tool for clubs in Canada.
  • Canadian Cancer Society: Raising awareness about cancer, as well as advocating for and supporting Canadians with cancer or with a history of cancer. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine and the Student Volunteer Bureau. Contact:
    • Main: 1-866-786-3934.
    • Provincial Office: Based out of St. John's; ccs@nl.cancer.ca; 709-753-6520 (local) or 1-888-753-6520 (toll-free).
    • Central Region: Based in Grand Falls-Windsor; centralregion@nl.cancer.ca; 709-489-5822 (local) or 1-866-545-9534 (toll-free).
    • Western Region: Based in Corner Brook; westernregion@nl.cancer.ca; 709-634-6542 (local) or 1-866-545-9533 (toll-free).
    • Newfoundland and Labrador Prostate Cancer Support Groups.
  • Canadian Red Cross: Provides support to those in need through services relating to health, food security, and social inclusion. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine and the Student Volunteer Bureau. Contact: 709-758-8400 (NL Provincial Office).
    • Search tool for services in Newfoundland and Labrador.
  • Choices for Youth: Supporting youth in the community with a focus on hard to reach populations like homeless youth. Connections with the Department of Political Science, the Faculty of Medicine, and the Student Volunteer Bureau. Contact:
    • Main office: 709-754-0446.
    • Ayon Shahed (partnership coordinator) - ashahed@choicesforyouth.ca.
  • Collective Interchange: Co-operative organization that addresses needs in the non-profit sector. Host site for summer student jobs. Contact: collectiveinterchange@gmail.com, 709-691-1215. 
  • Community Centre Alliance: Partnership of community centres helping better provide support in the St. John's area. Contact: contact@ccanl.ca, 709-753-5670.
    • Buckmaster Circle Community Centre: Connections with the Faculty of Medicine. Contact: Bonnie James (Executive Director) - bonnie@bmcc.nf.ca, 709-579-0718 ext. 221.
  • Community Employment Collaboration: Supporting job seekers in securing a job, employers in hiring employees, and career practitioners in networking with other practitioners and developing professionally. Connections with the Department of English, the Department of Political Science, the Department of Psychology, and the Faculty of Medicine. Contact: Elayne Greeley (Partnership Broker) - elayne@employmentcollaboration.ca, 709-765-2182.
  • Community Youth Network (CYN): Providing support to youth experiencing barriers (e. g. poverty), 34 in the province. 
    • Thrive CYN: St. John's, connections with the Department of Political Science and the Faculty of Medicine. Contact: info@thrivecyn.ca, 709-754-0536.
  • Food First NL: Ensuring access to healthy food for all of the people of Newfoundland and Labrador. Connections with the Department of Political Science, the Department of Psychology, and the Faculty of Medicine. Contact: info@foodfirstnl.ca, 709-237-4026.
  • The Gathering Place: Support centre in St. John’s for those dealing with homelessness, mental illness, addiction, poverty, etc. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine and Student Life. Contact: info@gatheringstjohns.ca, 709-753-3234.
  • Heart and Stroke Foundation: Promoting health, raising awareness about heart disease and stroke, and supporting those with a history of heart disease and/or stroke. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine and the Student Volunteer Bureau. Contact: 709-753-8521 (local) or 1-888-473-4636 (toll-free).
  • The John Howard Society of Newfoundland and Labrador: Providing support to ex-offenders to help them reintegrate into society. Connections with the Department of Political Science and the Student Volunteer Bureau. Contact: info@jhsnl.ca, 709-726-5500.
  • Municipalities Newfoundland and Labrador: Representing the interests of the 276 provincial municipalities. Connections with the Department of Political Science. Contact: 709-753-6820 (local) or 1-800-440-6536 (toll-free).
  • The Murphy Centre: Support in achieving one's educational and employment goals. Connections with the Department of Political Science and the Faculty of Medicine. Contact: academics@murphycentre.ca (Education Services), careerservices@murphycentre.ca (Employment Services), 709-579-6606.
  • The Newfoundland and Labrador Federation of Labour: Represents NL unions and workers and advocates for their rights. Connected with the Department of Political Science. Contact: fed@nlfl.nf.ca, 709-754-1660.
  • Newfoundland and Labrador Housing & Homelessness Network: Raising awareness about issues relating to housing and homelessness, as well as striving to address these issues. Connections with the Department of Psychology. Contact: info@nlhhn.org, 709-722-1030.
  • Refugee and Immigrant Advisory Council (RIAC): Providing support to newcomers and promoting multiculturalism in the province. Connections with the Department of Political Science, the School of Social Work, and the Student Volunteer Bureau. Contact: 709-754-4122.
  • SafetyNL: Promoting safe practices on the roads, in the workplace, and in the community. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine. Contact: info@safetyservicesnl.ca, 709-754-0210 (local) or 1-877-754-0210 (toll-free).
  • Stella's Circle: Helping those experiencing barriers find support, housing, and/or employment. Connections with the Faculty of Business Administration, the Faculty of Engineering and Applied Science, the School of Music, and the School of Social Work. Contact: info@StellasCircle.ca, 709-738-8390. 
  • The Vera Perlin Society: Providing support to those with developmental disabilities in the St. John's area. Connections with the Department of Political Science and the Student Volunteer Bureau. Contact: 709-739-6017, veraperlinsociety@nfld.net.
  • Waypoints: Providing support to children, youth, and families in terms of education, employment, health, housing, and social inclusion. Connected to the School of Social Work. Contact: Rick Kelly (Executive Director) - rickkelly@waypointsnl.ca, 709-368-6390; Hugo McCarthy (Project Manager) - 709-738-3255.
  • Women's Economic Council: National organization supporting the success of women. Host site for summer student jobs. Contact: info@WomensEconomicCouncil.ca, 1-800-584-0252.
    • Online portal with resources.
  • YMCA: International charity involved in supporting communities, promoting health, volunteering, etc. Connections with the Department of Psychology and the Student Volunteer Bureau. Contact: info@nl.ymca.ca, 709-726-9622.
    • YMCA of Western NL: Contact: 709-283-9622 (Bay St. George) or 709-639-9676 (Corner Brook).
    • Services in the North East Avalon area.
Previously Hosted Students - Government 
  • Department of Advanced Education, Skills, and Labour: Provincial department responsible for supporting education and employment. Connections with the Department of Political Science. Contact: aesl@gov.nl.ca.
    • Contacts by department.
  • Department of Education and Early Childhood Development: Provincial department responsible for supporting children and families through education, family resource centres, public libraries, etc. Connected with the Department of Psychology. Contact: education@gov.nl.ca, 709-729-5097.
  • Department of Health and Community Services: Department of the NL government responsible for providing health and community services, promoting health, and engaging in policy development. Connections to the Department of Political Science, the Faculty of Medicine, and the School of Pharmacy. Contact: healthinfo@gov.nl.ca, 709-729-4984 (Main).
    • Contact by Department.
    • Listing of services by community.
  • Human Rights Commission Newfoundland and Labrador: Helping ensure everyone's human rights are met within NL. Connections with the Department of Political Science. Contact: humanrights@gov.nl.ca, 709-729-2709 (local) or 1-800-563-5808 (toll-free).
  • Innu Nation: Representing the interests of the Innu of Labrador. Contact: 709-478-8755 (Natuashish).
    • Sheshatshiu Innu First Nation: Local council representing the Innu of Sheshatshiu. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine. Contact: 709-497-8522.
  • Nunatsiuvut Government: Regional government representing the Inuit people of Newfoundland and Labrador. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine. Contact: 709-922-2942 (Main).
    • Contacts by department.
  • Public Health Agency of Canada Atlantic: Atlantic regional office of PHAC, with programs focusing on population-level health such as disease prevention and programs focused on specific populations like Aboriginal communities and early childhood. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine. Contact: 1-844-280-5020 (National Line).
  • Public Service Commission: Department responsible for supporting workers and the workplace in the field of public service. Connection with the Department of Political Science. Contact: contactpsc@gov.nl.ca, 709-729-5810 (local) or 1-855-330-5810 (toll-free).
  • Women's Policy Office: Branch of the provincial government focusing on supporting women. Connections with the Department of Political Science, the Department of Psychology, the Faculty of Medicine, and the Student Volunteer Bureau. Contact: 709-729-5009.
    • Listing of regional status of women councils with contact information.
      Violence Prevention Initiative: Provincial initiative to address and prevent violence in the province with ten regional committees throughout the province. Contact: susanfurlong@gov.nl.ca, 709-729-5009.

  • Central Health: Providing health and community services in the central region of Newfoundland. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine.
  • College of Physicians and Surgeons of Newfoundland and Labrador: Responsible for regulating the medical profession in NL. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine. Contact: cpsnl@cpsnl.ca, 709-726-8546.
  • Definitions - Health and Wellness: Provides multidisciplinary health services such as physiotherapy, massage therapy, and personal training. Connected with the School of Human Kinetics and Recreation. Contact: info@definitionsonline.com, 709-576-3339.
  • Eastern Health: Providing health and community services in the eastern region of Newfoundland. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine, the Department of Psychology, Student Life, and the Student Volunteer Bureau.
    • Listing of services by community with contact information.
  • In Motion Health Centre, Inc.: Provides multidisciplinary health services such as occupational therapy, physiotherapy, and chiropractic services in St. John's. Connections with the School of Human Kinetics and Research. Contact: 709-747-5945 (Hamlyn Road) or 709-745-5945 (Topsail Road).
  • Labrador Grenfall Health: Providing health and community services in Labrador. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine. Contact: 1-855-897-2267 (main).
    • Listing of services by community with contact information.
  • Newfoundland and Labrador Centre for Health Information: Analyzing and storing health information for policy, research, and associated purposes. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine. Contact: 709-752-6000.
  • Newfoundland and Labrador College of Family Physicians: Represents the interests of NL family physicians and promotes the profession. Contact: Debbie Rideout (Administrator) - drideout@nlcfp.ca, 709-864-6566.
  • Newfoundland and Labrador Medical Association: Represents the interests of the medical profession in NL and promoting the profession. Connections with the Department of Political Science and the Faculty of Medicine. Contact: nlma@nlma.nl.ca, 709-726-7424 (local) or 1-800-563-2003 (toll-free).
  • Proactive Wellness and Fitness: Provides private health care services in Newfoundland and Labrador. Connections with the School of Human Kinetics and Research. Contact: ropewalk@proactivephysio.ca, 709-722-9165.
    • Locations and contacts throughout NL.
  • Western Health: Providing health and community services in the western region of Newfoundland. Connections with the Faculty of Medicine.
