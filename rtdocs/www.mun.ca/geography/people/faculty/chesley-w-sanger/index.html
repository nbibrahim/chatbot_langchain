Research Interests

  • Historical geography and marine resources
  • Scottish Arctic whale fishery, 1750-1801.
  • Newfoundland seal fishery, 19th and 20th centuries.
  • Newfoundland shore based whaling.
  • Northern and Scottish whaling.

Selected Publications

Categories (not mutually exclusive): i) Scottish Arctic Whaling; ii) Newfoundland and Labrador Whaling; iii) Newfoundland Harp Seal Fishery; iv) Newfoundland: History/Culture/Heritage; and v) Miscellaneous

Scottish Arctic Whaling

  • 2012 “The Rise of Scotland to a Position of Dominance in British Northern Whaling: 1802- 1840,” International Journal of Maritime History, 24, No. 1, 173-202.

  • 2000 “The Impact of the American Revolutionary War on Scottish Northern Whaling: The Dunbar Factor,” Northern Scotland, Vol. 20, 71-86.

  • 1988 “The Dundee-St. John’s Connection: Nineteenth Century Interlinkages between Scottish Arctic Whaling and the Newfoundland Seal Fishery,” Newfoundland Studies 4, No. 1, 1-26.
