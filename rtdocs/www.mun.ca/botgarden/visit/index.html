Visit the Garden

 

The Field Centre houses the Garden Gift Shop, offering local crafts, gardening books, nature-themed toys, and botanically-themed giftware, as well as the Postmaster's Garden Cafe, with their delicious lunch and brunch options. It is also the location for hosted art shows throughout the season.

Be sure to check out our self-guided audio tour as you explore our cultivated gardens.

Devoted to research and education, the Garden has been developed to display plants native to the province and cultivated plants suitable to the local climate and to provide access to a number of habitats through our system of nature trails.

 

Come explore and discover all that MUN Botanical Garden has to offer.
