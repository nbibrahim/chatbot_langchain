Garden Season Pass

Want VIP access? Get a season pass and have unlimited access to the Garden all season long! We have five choices, plus you can add a guest or grandchildren pass option.

We also offer discounted Combo Passes with our sister site: The Johnson GEO Centre. Prices are listed below as Garden Pass only price / Combo Pass price.  All prices are +HST.

Get Your 2023 Season Pass Here

Family ($60/$130): Year-round benefits for 2 designated card holders and all your accompanying children/grandchildren under the ages of 18.

Adult ($30/$60): Year-round benefits for 1 designated card holder.

Dual Adult ($50/$120): Year-round benefits for 2 designated adult card holders, living at the same address.

Senior ($20/$45): Year-round benefits for 1 designated card holder (60+).

Dual Senior ($30/$90): Year-round benefits for 2 designated senior card holders, living at the same address.

Plus Grandchildren ($10/$15): An add-on to your Season Pass. Admission for all your accompanying children/grandchildren between the ages of 5 - 18 years, any time you visit.

Guest Pass ($15/$20): Admission for one guest, per visit, when accompained by designated card holder. Cardholder benefits do not transfer to the guest visitor. Any of our season passes can avail of an add-on guest pass (can be used for any one guest that will accompany you on your visit, on as many visits as you would like). You can purchase up to 2 of these to add on.

Season Pass Holder Benefits
  • Free general admission to the Botanical Garden (For the Regular Admission season, May-November)
  • 10% discount and advance registration for Junior Naturalist Camps for Family Season Pass holders
  • 10% discount and advance registration for the Parent and Tot Programs for Family Season Pass holders
  • 10% discount on most workshops
  • 10% discount on Garden Gift Shop items (some exceptions apply)
  • Automatically added to the Botanical Garden email newsletter (The Bloom)
  • Access to "Season Pass holder only" events, programs, tours, lectures, etc.

Note:

  • Season pass admission does not apply to group tours, booked programs, or specially ticketed events.
  • 50% discount applies for current year passes purchased after September 1st.

 

For more information please contact Visitor Services at:
Tel: (709) 864-8590
Email: garden@mun.ca
