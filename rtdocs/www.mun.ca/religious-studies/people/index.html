Faculty

The Department of Religious Studies has a diverse and varied research profile. Most of our faculty have won external funding from granting agencies, including current SSHRC Insight Grants (Selby, Stephenson). 

Dr. Ayse Sule Akinturk

Postdoctoral Fellow
M.A., Manchester; M.A., Warwick; Ph.D., Carleton
Email: asakinturk@mun.ca

Research Interests: Islam and Muslim Religiosity, Secularism, Muslims and Muslim Heritage in Canada.

Dr. Michael DeRoche

Associate Professor
M.Th. Laval; M.A., Ph.D. McMaster
Office: 5010 Arts and Administration
Telephone: (709) 864-3539
Email: mderoche@mun.ca

Research Interests: Biblical studies, narratology, ancient Israelite religion, 2nd temple Judaism, early rabbinic Judaism

Dr. Patricia Dold

Currently Associate Dean of Curriculum and Programs, HSS

Associate Professor and Undergraduate Liaison Officer
M.A. Calgary; Ph.D. McMaster
Office: 5031 Arts and Administration
Telephone: (709) 864-4538
Email: pdold@mun.ca

Research Interests: Hindu religious texts, Hindu goddesses, women and religion

Dr. Jennifer Porter

Associate Professor
