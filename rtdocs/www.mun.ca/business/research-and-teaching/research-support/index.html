Research support

“Research is four things: brains with which to think, eyes with which to see, machines with which to measure and fourth, money.”
– Albert Szent-Gyorgyi

Research can be an expensive endeavour that requires an encouraging environment, institutional support and financial investment.

This section provides information on potential internal and external support and funding opportunities for faculty members, graduate students, and post-doctoral fellows.

If you know of any other websites with funding information that you think should be included, please email Mekaela Gulliver at mgulliver@mun.ca.
