Research Awards & Honours Deadlines

Memorial is very proud of the excellent research that is conducted within its faculties and schools. We believe that the individuals who participate in this research deserve recognition for the incredible work that they do through the various distinctions and honours that exist.

Below is a list of major internal, provincial, national and international research awards and prizes.  Please note that some of these awards are specifically targeted by the Awards Advisory Committee (AAC) and internal reviews are required.

If you wish to nominate an individual for one of these awards please contact:

  • the AAC member from your unit if the award is identified for internal review.
  • Ellen Steinhauer at 709-864-2651 or ellens@mun.ca for all other awards.
