Season Pass

Make Our Space, Your Place!

Join our inner circle.

There’s always something exciting happening at the Geo Centre – from exhibits to movie screenings to special expert lectures, and the best way to ensure you never miss a thing is a Geo Centre Season Pass.

When you become a season pass holder, you’ll receive invitations to members-only events, updates, and full time access to one of Canada’s premier Science Centres!

We also offer discounted Combo Passes with our sister site, Memorial University Botanical Garden. Prices are listed below as Geo Pass only price / Combo Pass price.  All prices are +HST.

Get Your 2023 Season Pass Here

Family ($85/$130): Year-round benefits for 2 designated card holders and all your accompanying children/grandchildren under the ages of 18.

Adult ($40/$60): Year-round benefits for 1 designated card holder.

Dual Adult ($75/$120): Year-round benefits for 2 designated adult card holders, living at the same address.

Senior ($35/$45): Year-round benefits for 1 designated card holder (60+).

Dual Senior ($65/$90): Year-round benefits for 2 designated senior card holders, living at the same address.

Plus Grandchildren ($10/$15): An add-on to your Season Pass. Admission for all your accompanying children/grandchildren between the ages of 5 - 18 years, any time you visit.

Guest Pass ($15/$20): Admission for one guest, per visit, when accompained by designated card holder. Cardholder benefits do not transfer to the guest visitor. Any of our season passes can avail of an add-on guest pass (can be used for any one guest that will accompany you on your visit, on as many visits as you would like). You can purchase up to 2 of these to add on. 

Your season pass benefits include:

  • Unlimited Regular Admission for the season
  • Free Admission to any Travelling Exhibit at Geo
  • Free Admission to Geo Public Lectures
  • Season Pass Holder-Only Events
  • 10% off at Geo Gifts (some exceptions apply)
  • 10% off Birthday Party Packages
  • 10% off selected Camps and Programs
  • 10% off 3D Movies
  • Free Admission (& Select Benefits) to over 350 Participating ASTC and CASC-ACCS Museums and Science Centres (Some restrictions apply. Contact participating facility for their details)
