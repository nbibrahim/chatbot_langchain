Policies & Procedures

All members of the university community have a responsibility to be aware of, and to comply with, university policy and procedures. You will find official non-academic policies and procedures on the university policy and procedures website. Other policies can be found in the university calendar or the collective agreement for Per Course Instructors.

Below you will find links to official university policies that are relevant to you and your work at Memorial University. Please review the policies and contact the department responsible listed at the end of each policy for further information or clarification.
