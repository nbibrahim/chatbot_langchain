Reunions

At the heart of MUNalum Days, reunions provide the perfect backdrop to reunite with friends and past professors, reminisce about the good times and stay up-to-date with what is happening at Memorial. Reunoins can be academic or affinity based, and can celebrate specific years or other important milestones. Check out below to see what reunions are coming up. If you don't see one specific to your group and want to help plan one, reach out to reunion@mun.ca.

*Click on each reunion title below for more detailed information. 

2023 Reunions

Frecker Institute 50th Anniversary | Dec. 9, 2023

The Department of Modern Languages, Literatures and Cultures invites all Frecker alumni and any Memorial University students interested in learning more about the Frecker Program to this Jubilee event.

Join us for a grand reunion and an exciting evening of program promotion. Let's reminisce, reconnect and grow the future of the Frecker program together!

While entry to this event is free, attendees must RSVP their intentions as we do have venue capacity restrictions. 

We hope to see you there! 

  • Date: Saturday, December 9, 2023
  • Time: 7:00 p.m. - 9:00 p.m.
    • Doors open at 6:30 p.m.
    • Speeches will commence at 7:30 p.m.
  • Location: Emera Innovation Exchange, Signal Hill Campus,
    100 Signal Hill Rd
  • Attire: Business casual
  • Parking: Complimentary for event attendees
  • Cost: Entry is free but you must RSVP to attend
  • Deadline to RSVP: 12:00 p.m. Newfoundland Time, Thursday, November 30, 2023
  • Notes:
    • There will be light refreshments and hot and cold hors d'oeuvres served throughout the evening;
    • There will be a cash bar;
    • The Sheraton Hotel is offering a discounted room rate for event attendees. To avail, you can click here OR go to www.marriott.com and choose 'Special Rates', then 'Corporate' and enter the code YMH; and
    • If you have any questions, please direct them to languages@mun.ca.

CLICK HERE TO RSVP NOW!

We would also like to take the opportunity to acknowledge the financial contributions of the Government of Newfoundland & Labrador and the Government of Canada, without whose support we would not have been able to offer this opportunity to attendees of the Frecker Program.

Reunions on the Horizon

Memorial University Centennial Celebration | 2025

Can you believe it, in 2025 Memorial University (College) will celebrate its 100th anniversary. While we are pretty early in the planning stages, please know that there will be a celebration. 

Grenfell Campus Golden Celebration | 2025

Another great milestone of 2025 is the 50th anniversary of Grenfell Campus. While it's too early to say what is being planned, we wanted to make sure this was on your radar.

 

Reunion Planning

Plan a Reunion

Memorial University encourages alumni to reunite through reunions. If you’re interested in getting together with your classmates and colleagues and didn’t see a gathering in our upcoming reunions section, we’re here to help. Check out our resources for planning a reunion. Learn more!

Travel and Accommodations

If you’re traveling from away, relive your campus days and stay with memorial. Check out all the awesome things to do while you’re here. Learn more!

Reunion Giving

Use your reunion to encourage reunion giving. As you reconnect with Memorial and celebrate your experiences, you can have a direct impact on our students. Learn more!
