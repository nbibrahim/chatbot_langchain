History
Luminus (est. 1971) is Memorial University’s alumni magazine. Published once a year, it is distributed to: alumni, friends, supporters of the university, campus departments and divisions, and other key stakeholders. Each issue of Luminus contains 32 full-colour pages.

Readership
Memorial alumni are a diverse group of leaders who make important professional and civic contributions to their communities. Luminus magazine provides advertisers with direct access to a unique, targeted demographic that enables their own strategic outreach and positions their brand as a Memorial partner.
