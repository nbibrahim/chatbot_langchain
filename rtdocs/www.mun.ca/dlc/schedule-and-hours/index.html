Tips & Notes

Labnet account required
You will need to have or get a LabNet account in order to use the computer in the DLC. 

Closed for Midterm Break
The DLC will be closed during mid-term break week (October 9-10, inclusive) and no conversation classes will take place on those days. We re-open for regular hours on Wednesday, October 11.

---

Wednesday, October 4th follows MONDAY's schedule on this date only.

Thursday, October 12 follows TUESDAY's schedule on this date only.

Friday, November 17 follows MONDAY's schedule on this date only.

---

Pick a time and stick to it
Students are encouraged to select a regular time during the week when it is convenient to come to the DLC, and stick to that time as much as possible. In this way you get into a study routine that involves being able ask the monitors for help.

What to do if there's a class being taught
If there is a class scheduled during the time you wish to use the DLC, we ask that you allow the class to be seated first and then, if there are any remaining computers available, you are welcome to do your individual lab work.
