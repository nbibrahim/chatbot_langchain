We are committed to supporting students in every way and have a range of tools, services and resources to help you succeed.

Not a current Memorial student? What are you waiting for?

Undergraduate

Academics are half the story. There is a wide range of services available as you complete your journey at Memorial. Undergraduate students on campus at Grenfell, the Marine Institute and St. John's are supported by counselling and career services, health and wellness programs, academic advising and access to recreation facilities. The student life experience is rich with opportunities to participate in the campus community.

Learn more

Graduate

Graduate life at Memorial is an experience like no other. Enjoy the adventure of living on the edge while studying at the most easterly university in Canada. Work-life balance is an important part of your graduate experience. In between studying, be sure to find time to explore on and off campus.

Learn more

Online

Education that fits your life is what online learning at Memorial is all about. Whether you are taking a single course or completing an entire program online, we are here to make sure your experience with online learning at Memorial is both enjoyable and rewarding. We offer an array of student services, from extended hours of technical support to access to one of the best university libraries in Canada.

Learn more
