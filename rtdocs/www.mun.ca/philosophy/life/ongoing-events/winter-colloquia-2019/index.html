Winter Colloquia 2019

The Winter Colloquium Series, 2019:

  • "Discourse, justification and education: Jurgen Habermas on moral epistemology and dialogical conditions of moral justification and rightness" Dr. Walter Okshevsky, Education and Philosophy, Memorial University. Tuesday, Feb. 12th 2019, 3:30-4:45pm, SN 2105

  • "You Can’t Always Get (Exactly) What You Want: Socrates’ Eros Deinos for Protagoras" Dr. Suma Rajiva, Philosophy, Memorial University Tuesday, Feb. 26th 2019, 3:30-4:45pm, SN 2105

  • "In a Familiar Voice: The Dominant Role of Women in Shaping Canadian Policy on Medical Assistance in Dying" Dr. Daryl Pullman (Community Health and Humanities) Thursday, March 14, 2019, 3:30-4:45, SN 2105.

  • "Hegel on Romantic Art and Modernity" Visiting Speaker, Dr. John Russon (Philosophy, University of Guelph) Tuesday, March 19, 2019, 3:30-4:45, SN 2105

  • "Nussbaum’s 'Danger': Missing the Mirror.Aristotle on saving phenomena... not concept. " Dr. John Scott, Philosophy, Memorial University, Tuesday, March 26th. 2019, 3:30-4:45, SN 2105

  • "Touching the Unbounded with Your Thought; Exploring the Concept of The Infinite in Cartesian Metaphysics." Gil Shalev, Philosophy, Memorial University, Tuesday, April 2nd, 2019, 3:30-4:45, SN 2105
