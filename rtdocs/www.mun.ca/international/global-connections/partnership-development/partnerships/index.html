Partnerships Overview

Memorial University has linkages with more than 100 post-secondary institutions around the globe. In the table below, we have a short-list of those elibigle for exchange.

As agreements are signed and renewed on a weekly basis, the most up-to-date list is available from your mobility coordinators.

Please use this as guidance for determining your short-list of potential locations for your exchange.
