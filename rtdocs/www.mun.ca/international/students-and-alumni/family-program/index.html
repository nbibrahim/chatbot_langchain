Family Program

 

There are over 300 International students with a spouse and/or children at Memorial University. The Family Program provides a holistic approach to supporting International students and their families, by providing community programs, one-to-one supports and community referrals. The goal is to provide a sense of community both on and off campus for families. 

We invite you to take a look at all the comprehensive resources we have compiled for you and your family.

  • Regular Family Programming (hosted by the Internationalization Office)
  • Information and Supports for Spouses/Partners of International Students
  • Information and Supports for Dependent Children of International Students
  • Family Handbook
  • Pregnancy Resource

The Family Advisor can also provide tailored support for families with personal, financial, or other family concerns.

To register as a new family or stay up to date on our family programs, please fill out the Family Intake Form found here.
