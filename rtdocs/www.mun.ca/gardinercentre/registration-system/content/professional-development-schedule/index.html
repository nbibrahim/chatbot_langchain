Never Stop Learning

Gardiner Centre’s suite of over 100 practical and engaging short courses and certificate programs will advance your professional goals, stimulate personal growth, and inspire change.

Did you know that when you register 5 employees from the same organization in a one or two day course together, the 6th employee is free? Browse our list of open enrollment training below to get started on your professional development pathway.

Need more information to decide what's right for you? Contact our Open Enrollment Partnerships Lead, Melissa Kelly, or call her at 709-864-8883 to find the best solution for your professional development needs.

Looking for something more custom? Visit our Custom Training for Employers page or connect with our Custom Training Partnerships Lead, Susan Arscott, or call her at 709-864-3596 and start planning your custom training!

Not sure if your professional development needs are open enrollment or custom? Contact our Professional Development Advisor, Pearce Burton or call him at 709-864-2131 to discuss.
