Past Students

Chaired by Dr. Ban Younghusband, the Discipline of Genetics formed in 1999. Although it is a relatively small and new Discipline, we have had good success recruiting additional Faculty members and we have a strong commitment to supporting our graduate students. Below is a catalog of those students that have graduated from the Human Genetics program to date. As can be observed, they have gone on to become successful clinicians, medical researchers and educators who reside in Newfoundland & Labrador and throughout the globe. We anticipate many more successful graduates in the future.
