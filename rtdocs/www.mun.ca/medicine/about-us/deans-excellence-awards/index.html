Dean's Excellence Awards

To promote and acknowledge members of the Faculty of Medicine for excellence in research, teaching and service, the faculty has launched the 2023 Dean's Excellence Awards. Nomination packages should be submitted via email to the Chair of the Medicine Awards Committee no later than November 30, 2023.

Awards are offered to junior and senior-career faculty – both clinical and non-clinical as it relates to research and teaching  – who have demonstrated recent exceptional contributions.

Awards are offered to non-academic staff in recognition of work and contributions that exceed the expectation of their positions.

Senior Faculty Research Awards

Two awards for outstanding research are available to faculty members holding tenure or tenure-track appointments who are currently beyond seven years in their first faculty appointment. One award is available per category: clinical or non-clinical research.

Senior Faculty Award Terms of Reference (PDF)

Junior Faculty Research Awards

Two awards for outstanding research are available to faculty members holding tenure or tenure-track appointments who are currently between one to seven years in their first faculty appointment. One award is available per category: clinical or non-clinical research.

Junior Faculty Award Terms of Reference (PDF)

Senior Faculty Teaching Awards

An award for outstanding teaching and/or educational scholarship may be given to clinical or non-clinical senior faculty members holding part-time, tenure or tenure-track appointments who are currently beyond seven years in their first faculty appointment.

Senior Teaching Award Terms of Reference (PDF)

Junior Faculty Teaching Awards

An award for outstanding teaching and/or educational scholarship may be given to clinical or non-clinical junior faculty members holding part-time, tenure or tenure-track appointments who are within seven years in their first faculty appointment.

Junior Teaching Award Terms of Reference (PDF)

Max House Teaching Awards

The Max House Teaching Award for Excellence in Continuing Medical Education/Continuing Professional Development (CME/CPD) is for outstanding contribution of an individual to advancing CME/CPD planning, teaching and/or scholarly research for physicians and health professionals in the province of Newfoundland and Labrador and beyond.

The Max House Teaching Award

Community Member Health Champion Award

This award recognizes the work and contributions of individuals or groups based outside Memorial University in advocating and acting for health promotion.

Community Member Health Champion Award Terms of Reference (PDF)

Service Excellence Awards

Three awards for outstanding service are available to non-academic staff (Management, Administrative, Technical) who are working in a division/discipline/unit.

Service Excellence Award Terms of Reference (PDF)
