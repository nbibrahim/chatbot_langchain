Student Responsibilities

All medical students have responsibilities, including but not limited to the following:

  • Check D2L and MUN email daily for updates and announcements;
  • Attend class/duties, be on time and perform the work of the class;
  • Take advantage of the available learning opportunities;
  • Display professional attitudes and behaviours as outlined on the Professionalism page. 
  • Report absences to the UGME office as well as your principal faculty/preceptor during clinical learning experiences.
    • Phases 1, 2 and 3: via email to ugme.phasesched@med.mun.ca or via phone at 709-864-6305 or toll free 1-855-884-8463
  • Seek help early from faculty, the Office of Learner Well-being and Success or the UGME office;
  • Know your rights and be familiar with the Regulations and Policies for Memorial University and the Faculty of Medicine;
  • Voice general concerns through class representatives;
  • Participate in peer, course and instructor evaluation.
