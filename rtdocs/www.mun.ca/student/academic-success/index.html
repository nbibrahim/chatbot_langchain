The keys to academic excellence.

Memorial University offers a broad range of academic supports to help you throughout your university career. 

New Student Orientation and Welcome

Everything you need to know to start your university experience on the right foot.

Office of the Registrar

The Office of the Registrar provides academic support services and systems to prospective and current students - from initial contact to application, registration, and graduation.

Academic Advising Centre

The Academic Advising Centre serves prospective, first-year, and undeclared students, as well as students transitioning between academic programs. The Academic Advising Centre is dedicated to helping you with your academic questions and concerns.

Bookstore

Memorial University's Bookstore offers textbooks, school supplies, apparel, giftware and much more. Pop in to the second floor of the University Centre for a visit or have a look online.

Libraries

Memorial has six library locations with specialized resources, skilled staff and librarians, and an incredible wealth of information to support teaching, learning, and research.

The Commons

The Commons Computing Support Area, located on the main floor of the QEII Library, is a central computing resource to facilitate research writing, presentation creation, and more.

Help Centres

Memorial has a number of Help Centres to assist you with your studies, staffed by senior students who have extensive experience with that subject.

Online Learning (CITL)

CITL - the Centre for Innovation in Teaching and Learning - is Memorial's lead on the delivery of the university's online education offerings, the use of educational technologies in teaching and learning, and the provision of educator development.

MUNUp

MUNUp is an online tools and resources hub to help students succeed while learning remotely. With transition and orientation programs, services, virtual events and community engagement opportunities, the skyâ€™s the limit.

Academic Success Centre

The Academic Success Centre is Memorial's virtual hub for information, tools, programs, and events that will help you succeed in your learning. 

Experiential Learning

Memorial offers many high-impact practices throughout all faculties, schools, and campuses, helping you to put theory into practice.
